Learning by Doing
Build the Crew Forward Project Plan
Scenario

Your team has been assigned to plan and deliver a company-wide event that will bring together employees from different departments to strengthen collaboration and alignment.

The project involves coordinating logistics, preparing materials, and organizing the program. Your team is responsible for planning and executing the initiative from start to finish.

Your Task

Create a project plan using the tools from this Deep Dive. Show:

  • What needs to be done
  • When it should happen
  • Who is responsible
Submit as Word, PDF, or clear photos (max 5 pages).
Plan Using These Tools
WBS – Define WHAT must be delivered.
Break the project into deliverables and work packages.
NetPlan – Show WHEN tasks happen.
Identify sequence, dependencies, and the critical path.
RACI – Assign WHO owns each task.
Responsible • Accountable • Consulted • Informed
Tip: If details are missing, make reasonable assumptions. A clear plan matters more than a perfect one.
